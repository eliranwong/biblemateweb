# **Bible Promises Guide**

This tool organizes God's promises throughout the Bible into thematic collections, making it easy to find encouragement, hope, and assurance in Scripture.

## **What are Bible Promises?**

The Bible Promises tool is a curated collection of God's promises organized by topic. Each collection groups related promises together, showing you all the relevant Bible passages where God makes commitments, assurances, or guarantees to His people.

## **What You'll Find**

The tool includes organized collections of promises on topics such as:
- **Provision and Care**: God's promises to meet your needs
- **Protection and Safety**: Promises of God's watchful care
- **Presence**: God's promise to be with you
- **Guidance**: Promises of divine direction
- **Strength**: Promises of power and endurance
- **Peace**: Promises of inner tranquility
- **Healing**: Physical and spiritual restoration
- **Forgiveness**: Promises of mercy and grace
- **Eternal Life**: Promises about salvation and heaven
- **Prayer**: Promises about answered prayer
- And many more...

## **How to Use Bible Promises**

1. **Open the Tool**: Select "Promises" from the tools menu.
2. **Browse Collections**: Scroll through the list of promise categories.
3. **Expand a Collection**: Click on any category to see its contents.
4. **View Promises**: Each promise is displayed with its Bible reference.
5. **Click References**: Click any Bible reference to read the full passage.

- Determine if the promise applies to your situation

- Act in faith based on the promise

### **4. Stand on the Promise**

- Memorize key promise verses
- Recall promises during trials
- Share promises with others
- Let promises strengthen your faith

## **Understanding Different Types of Promises**

### **Unconditional Promises**

Made without conditions:
- God's covenant with Noah (never flood earth again)
- Abrahamic covenant
- New Covenant in Christ
- Promises based on God's character

### **Conditional Promises**

Require obedience or faith:
- "If you seek me, you will find me"
- "If you forgive others, you will be forgiven"
- "If you humble yourselves and pray..."
- Blessings tied to obedience

### **Universal Promises**

Apply to all people:
- "Whoever believes in him shall not perish"
- "Whosoever shall call on the name of the Lord"
- Gospel promises to all nations

### **Specific Promises**

Made to individuals or groups:
- Promises to Israel
- Promises to the Church
- Promises to specific people (David, Mary, etc.)

### **Already/Not Yet Promises**

Partially fulfilled, awaiting completion:
- Kingdom of God (now and future)
- New creation (begun, not complete)
- Resurrection (Christ's and ours)

## **Theological Foundations**

### **God's Faithfulness**

- God cannot lie (Titus 1:2)
- His promises are "Yes and Amen" in Christ (2 Cor 1:20)
- What He promises, He fulfills (Rom 4:21)
- His faithfulness endures forever

### **Christ as Fulfillment**

- All promises find their "yes" in Jesus
- Old Testament promises fulfilled in Christ
- New covenant promises based on His work
- Future promises guaranteed by resurrection

### **Holy Spirit as Guarantee**

- Spirit is the deposit/down payment (Eph 1:13-14)
- Seals believers until redemption
- Guarantees future fulfillment
- Empowers believers to trust promises
