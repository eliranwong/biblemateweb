# **Bible Timelines Guide**

This tool displays visual timeline images that illustrate the chronological flow of biblical events, periods, and history through detailed charts and maps.

## **What are Bible Timelines?**

The Bible Timelines tool provides access to a collection of professionally designed visual timelines that show the sweep of biblical history. These timelines use images, charts, and maps to help you understand when events occurred and how they relate to each other chronologically.

## **How to Use Bible Timelines**

1. **Open the Tool**: Select "Timelines" from the tools menu.
2. **Browse Periods**: Use the dropdown or navigation arrows to select a time period.
3. **View Timeline**: The timeline image loads for that period.
4. **Toggle View Modes**: Switch between "Fit Width" and "Actual Size" views.
5. **Navigate**: Use Previous/Next buttons or the dropdown to move between timelines.

## **View Modes**

### **Fit Width Mode (Default)**

- Timeline scales to fit your screen width
- Entire timeline visible without scrolling
- Best for overview and quick reference
- Maintains aspect ratio
- Title displayed below image

**Best for:**
- Getting the big picture
- Quick scanning of events
- Presentations or teaching
- Comparing multiple timelines

### **Actual Size Mode**

- Timeline displays at original high resolution
- May require scrolling to see entire image
- Shows all details clearly
- Best for reading small text
- No title overlay

**Best for:**
- Reading detailed text
- Examining dates and connections
- Studying specific events
- Printing or exporting

**Toggle between modes with the "Fit Width" switch.**

## **Understanding Dating Systems**

### **Old Testament Dates**

- Often approximate
- May vary by scholarly tradition
- Based on biblical genealogies and records
- Cross-referenced with ancient Near Eastern history

### **New Testament Dates**

- Generally more precise
- Based on Roman historical records
- Cross-referenced with Josephus and other historians
- Some debate on specific years (e.g., Jesus' birth year)

### **BC/AD System**

- BC: Before Christ
- AD: Anno Domini (Year of Our Lord)
- Dates count down to 1 BC, then up from AD 1
- No year zero in traditional system
